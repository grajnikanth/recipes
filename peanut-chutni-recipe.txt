Peanut and Putnalu Chetni for Idli 

In a frying pan put oil and put (4) cut chilies. Cut chilies into 
two pieces using the long chilies. Put jeelakarra in the fry pan. 
just fry for a minute. In a grinder put one glass fried peanuts 
and one glass putnalu. Add half small bag of andi gobbari. Add 
chintha pandu to this mixture and add the fried chilies aswell. 
Add two spoons of salt. But each spoon of salt should only have 
small amount of salt. Grind it once and add one cup of water. 
Grind more and add water as needed to make the chutney as liquid 
as needed. Thatâ€™s it.
